YUGEN RANCH GREENHOUSE

A farm to nourish the body, mind, and spirit of our community

Mission

To produce nourishing local vegetables to feed our families, friends, and communities so that we can all bring our best to the world. The greenhouse will dramatically improve the quality of food available to the broader community.

Vision

Jackson Hole offers world-class outdoor activities and connection to nature, but has limited supply of local fresh vegetables year-round given the harsh climate. We are building a local greenhouse to produce all the veggies we need to create amazing meals and give back to the community.
