Jackson Hole, Wyoming   |   Thursday, August 18, 2022

LOCATION UPDATE

Jackson, WY

We are so excited to welcome you to Jackson on August 18th for the Prime Movers Lab Summer Retreat 2022!

The day will include a scenic river float in the morning (shuttle provided from Jackson), lunch in town, and evening event. See the agenda, as well as other useful information for the event below.

If you have any questions, please reach out to Nicolette or Daniel.

SUMMER RETREAT AGENDA

Thursday, August 18th

8:00am / 8:10am: Buses depart Jackson for Moose
Buses will depart promptly from the two stops listed below to take us to the shuttle vans in Moose, WY (the entrance of Grand Teton National Park). Please arrive early and be ready to load busses for an on-time departure!
• 8:00am : Busses depart the base of Snow King Mountain
• 8:10am : Busses depart the Jackson Home Ranch Welcome Center

If you would prefer to drive your own vehicle to the shuttles in Moose (vs. taking the bus Jackson to Moose) look for a white "Solitude Float Trips" van in the designated parking area. Detailed driving directions can be found at the very end of this email.

9:00 AM: Shuttle vans depart Moose, WY
Shuttle vans will depart promptly from Moose to take us to the starting point on the river, about a 20 minute drive.

9:30 AM: Start of the Scenic River Float in Grand Teton National Park
At this time we will receive a safety briefing and split up into groups of 10-12 people per boat. This section of the river is extremely mellow and we will not encounter any rapids. We suggest bringing sun protection as well as a wind breaker or other warmer layer as weather on the river can change quickly.

11:30 AM: Float ends - buses return to The Welcome Center & Snow King Mountain

12:00 PM: Lunch at StillWest Brewery & Grill
After our morning river float we will sit and enjoy lunch at the StillWest Brewery, located at the base of Snow King Mountain where the bus will drop us off.

2:00 PM: Free Time

3:30 PM: Meet at The Cloudveil Hotel in the Lupine Room
The Cloudveil Hotel is a newly built hotel that is located right off the town square. We will re-gather at 3:30pm and begin a panel discussion soon after.

4:00 PM: Panel Discussion in the Lupine Room

5:00 PM: One Ton Pig & Cocktails on the Rooftop
After the panel we will transition to the Cloudveil Hotel Rooftop where we will enjoy a live Bluegrass band, cocktails, and hors d'oeuvres while watching the sunset. Cowboy boots and swing dancing encouraged.

8:00 PM: Dinner in the Lupine Room of The Cloudveil Hotel
Once the band finishes we will transition to a seated dinner indoors.

ABOUT THE SUMMER RETREAT

Our Summer Retreat is designed to be an opportunity for our ecosystem to come together, get in touch with nature, and to celebrate the journey that we are all on. This year we will host a one-day event in Jackson, WY on Thursday, August 18th. Our team, founders, and LPs will all be in attendance, +1s are welcome.

DRESS CODE - CASUAL & WESTERN

The full-day event will be centered around nature and outdoor activities, so we encourage everyone to dress comfortably and casually. For the float trip we suggest packing river shoes or sandals, sun hat, and a windbreaker (in case it's windy or cloudy). For the evening we suggest wearing your dancing boots and your finest western clothes.

GETTING TO AND AROUND JACKSON

We suggest flying into Jackson (JAC) on Wednesday, August 17th (or earlier), and staying through the weekend to explore all that this beautiful area has to offer. If you plan to stay for the weekend, you might think about renting a car through one of the nation-wide agencies that operate here in Jackson. A car will allow you to travel to Grand Teton National Park, Yellowstone National Park, and Astoria Hot Springs. All of the activities that we will be sponsoring will be accessible via walking, biking, or the town bus.

ACCOMMODATIONS

Summer is a very busy time of year in Jackson, so we suggest booking accommodations as soon as possible. Especially for those who do not plan on renting a car, we recommend staying in the town of Jackson (rather than Wilson or Teton Village) because there are more restaurants and activities accessible by walking.

Recommended Hotels:
The Cloudveil Hotel (hosting Thursday's activities)
Snow King Resort (hosting the PML team)
Most affordable options: Lexington, Anvil Hotel, Ranch Inn Motel

SCENIC FLOAT TRIP

Driving Directions to the Moose, WY

Recommended Hotels:

While we recommend taking the bus from Jackson with the rest of the group, if you would prefer to meet us at the shuttles in Moose please follow the directions below:
• From Jackson drive towards Moose, WY, north on Cache St which becomes North US-191 for 12 miles.
• 4 miles past the turn-off for the Jackson Hole airport turn left at Moose Junction onto Teton Park Road.
• Proceed 0.5 miles and cross over the Snake River.
• As soon as you cross the bridge turn right at the four-way stop into the designated parking area.
• Look for a white van with “Solitude Float Trips” on the side.
