ABOUT THE SUMMER RETREAT

Our Summer Retreat is designed as an opportunity for our team to come together in a place where it's easy for us to reconnect with nature and ourselves. This year we are experimenting with a new format, new location, and new experiences! Yugen Ranch is about an hour south of Jackson, so please take that into consideration when thinking about your travel logistics.

Getting to Jackson & Yugen Ranch
Arrivals: Please fly into Jackson (JAC) on Sunday the 14th or Monday the 15th by 5pm, we have dinner scheduled at 7pm at the Ranch on Monday.
Ground transportation from Jackson to Yugen Ranch is TBD.
Departures: Please fly out on Friday mid-morning or afternoon, avoid the 7am flight, we will not have transportation options to get you to the airport early enough to make this flight.

ACCOMMODATIONS

There are some bedrooms available in the main house and guest house, but most of the team will be staying in one of the glamping tents that we will be setting up with a partner company. If you have a strong preference to stay in the main/guest house, please reach out to Daniel directly.
