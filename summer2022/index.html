ABOUT THE SUMMER RETREAT

Our Summer Retreat is designed to be an opportunity for folks to come together, get in touch with nature, and to celebrate the journey that we are all on. This year we will host a one-day event at Dakin and Gabrielle's Yugen Ranch on Thursday, August 18th. Our team, founders, and LPs will all be in attendance, spouses are welcome.

GETTING TO YUGEN RANCH

We suggest flying into Jackson (JAC) on Wednesday, August 17th, and staying through the weekend to explore all that this beautiful area has to offer. If you plan to stay for the weekend, we recommend renting a car through one of the nation-wide agencies that operate here in Jackson. For Thursday, we will be arranging a large passenger bus to transport everyone between Jackson and Yugen Ranch, at the times specified above. If you will have a rental car and would prefer to drive yourself to Yugen Ranch, you are welcome to do so.

ACCOMMODATIONS

Summer is a very busy time of year in Jackson, so we suggest booking accommodations as soon as possible. Especially for those who do not plan on renting a car, we recommend staying in the town of Jackson (rather than Wilson or Teton Village) because there are more restaurants and activities accessible by walking.

DRESS CODE - CASUAL!

The full-day event will be centered around nature and outdoor activities, so we encourage everyone to dress comfortably and casually. We suggest arriving in activewear, with a change of clothes for the evening. Changing areas will be available.
