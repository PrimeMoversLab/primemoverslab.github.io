NET — May 12, 2020

UNMC, COVAXX Partner To Develop Large-Scale COVID-19 Antibody Testing

The University of Nebraska Medical Center has partnered with a New York-based company known for vaccine development to work toward effective COVID-19 antibody testing and processing. COVAXX is a subsidiary of United Biomedical. Dr. Steven Hinrichs, Chairman of UNMC’s Pathology and Microbiology Department, said antibody testing relies on blood samples to determine whether a person has had an immune response to COVID-19, rather than detect if the virus is present. He said the tests are an important step in the development of a vaccine for the virus.
