Bryan Bauw Head of Manufacturing & Partner

Bryan is Head of Manufacturing and Partner at Prime Movers Lab. He provides manufacturing due diligence on potential investments, engages in a wide spectrum of advanced technology and manufacturing evaluation, and uses his experience with international methodologies and operations to support Prime Movers as they scale their manufacturing capability.

Bryan has long been passionate about leading advanced manufacturing teams and continuous improvement cultures to develop and deliver groundbreaking products and technologies. Prior to joining Prime Movers Lab, Bryan managed manufacturing operations and programs for Fortune 500 and FTSE 100 companies. His drive for excellence earned the teams' top Quality and Delivery awards and accreditations from his customers. Bryan earned his Masters degree in Industrial and Operations Engineering from the University of Michigan.
