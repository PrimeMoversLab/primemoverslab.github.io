Who We Are

Our team has developed in-depth academic scientific research, commercialized novel intellectual property, scaled significant enterprises, and mentored renowned scientists on their entrepreneurial journey.

✕
Dakin Sloss Founder & General Partner

Dakin Sloss is Founder and General Partner of Prime Movers Lab. He also served as Founder and CEO of Tachyus, which is the leading prescriptive analytics company in the oil and gas industry. Tachyus’ breakthrough technology synthesizes machine learning and physics to dramatically increase production and reduce costs.

Dakin also co-founded OpenGov, where as CEO he recruited the core team, secured millions in angel funding and venture capital, arranged the initial pilot partnerships, and launched the world’s most advanced smart government platform. Before OpenGov, he built California Common Sense, the premiere California open data and government watchdog non-profit. Dakin earned a BS in Mathematics from Stanford University in 2011. In 2016, Dakin was recognized as the featured Forbes 30 Under 30 energy entrepreneur. In 2017, he was named as a San Francisco Business Times 40 Under 40 honoree.

✕
Suzanne Fletcher Venture Partner

Suzanne is a Venture Partner with Prime Movers Lab. She has 20 years of experience working in the technology and investment space. Most recently, she spent five years at StartX, the Stanford University focused entrepreneur community, managing the Stanford-StartX Fund, and working directly with hundreds of entrepreneurs. Prior to that, she spent ten years doing secondary private equity investing. Suzanne started her career at Morgan Stanley in the Technology Financial Sponsors Group.

As Fund Manager at StartX, Suzanne oversaw $175M of direct investment in 300 start-ups across 500+ rounds, from seed through Series D, in industries ranging across Enterprise, Consumer, Hardware, and Medical. She earned an MBA from Stanford University and a BS in Finance and Information Systems from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania magna cum laude.

Suzanne lives in Silicon Valley, she is happily married to an entrepreneur, and is a mom to human twins & lot of pets!

Brandon Simmons Venture Partner

Brandon Simmons is Chief Operating Officer of Tachyus, where he leads global business development, customer success, and operations. Brandon is also a sought-after coach and advisor to startups and non-profits, including as a mentor at Berkeley SkyDeck.

Brandon has more than a decade of experience as an angel investor with several successful exits. Previously, Brandon was an attorney at a leading global law firm, where his experience included significant work for Silicon Valley companies and investors as well as projects in the Middle East, Africa, Asia, Latin America, and Europe. Brandon served as law clerk to Chief Judge JL Edmondson of the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit and earned degrees from Stanford Law School and the University of California, Berkeley.

Tony Robbins Business Strategist & Partner

Tony Robbins is an entrepreneur, best-selling author, philanthropist, and the world’s #1 life and business strategist.

For over 4 decades, more than 50 million people have enjoyed the warmth, humor, and transformational power of his business and personal development events. Mr. Robbins is the chairman of a holding company comprised of 50 privately held businesses with combined sales exceeding $5 billion a year. He has been honored by Accenture as one of the “Top 50 Business Intellectuals in the World”; by Harvard Business Press as one of the “Top 200 Business Gurus”; and by American Express as one of the “Top Six Business Leaders in the World” to coach their entrepreneurial clients. Fortune’s 2014 cover article named him the “CEO Whisperer,” and he has been named in the top 50 of Worth Magazine’s 100 most powerful people in global finance for two consecutive years.

Decker Cunov Executive Coach & Partner

Decker Cunov is a Partner and Executive Coach for Prime Movers Lab. As a mediator and coach he challenges organizations to attain extraordinary levels of teamwork and performance. His 1:1 coaching focuses on CEOs effectively empowering themselves, their executives and organizations. Decker has also served as mediator for over $1.3 billion worth of business deals.

Decker previously coached the Founders Fund team and their most promising portfolio companies. He has coached startups from their first pitch, through board-level conflicts, to acquisition and even to starting again. Prior to his consulting, Decker co-founded a one-of-a-kind residential homeless program in San Francisco called Arkhouse. Later he founded Authentic World and The Integral Center, leading programs on advanced communication skills and certifying coaches around the world. He began his career team-building and mediating as a medic in the military, preparing soldiers for leadership roles.

Dan Slomski Principal Engineer & Partner

Dan Slomski is a Partner and Principal Engineer for Prime Movers Lab. As a systems engineer, he performs in-depth due diligence into potential investments and supports portfolio companies in analyzing and overcoming mission critical technical design and execution challenges. Dan has designed patented systems for a range of applications including biotech, energy, consumer electronics, and materials.

Dan previously served as a Systems Engineer for Gener8 designing, researching and prototyping electro-mechanical systems primarily for biotech applications. Prior to Gener8, as Lead Hardware Engineer at Tachyus, he invented a multi-phase flow fluid analyzer and built an elite team to design and field trial the device. In earlier consulting engagements, Dan created a machine vision camera solution, a rugged computer case, and numerous industrial art projects. He began his career at Thermo Fisher Scientific, where he developed an analyzer to perform compositional element analysis of drilled material. Dan earned his BS in physics from Penn State University.

JJ Moi Creative Director & Partner

JJ Moi is a Partner and Creative Director for Prime Movers Lab. As a designer, he evaluates the products of potential investments and engages deeply with portfolio companies to develop differentiated branding, marketing, and design strategies. JJ has created brand identities, websites, web applications, ad campaigns, and physical products across startups, large institutions, governments, and design agencies.

Previously, JJ was the Creative Director at OpenGov, where he was the lead designer for the world's leading open government platform. Before OpenGov, he supported world-leading brands in their design and branding efforts through multiple top-tier design agencies. He continues to be a design mentor at Out of Office Hours and Designlab teaching aspiring designers about the tricks of the trade. He has also previously served as an innovation specialist at 18F, a US government innovation laboratory. JJ completed postgraduate studies in New Media from Academy of Art University and earned a BFA in Industrial Design from Rangsit University.

Anton Brevde Chief of Staff

Anton Brevde is Chief of Staff of Prime Movers Lab. He supports Dakin and the rest of the Prime Movers Lab team across all facets of the business. He utilizes his seven years of startup experience to help the team evaluate the market for potential investments and to support portfolio companies.

Previously, Anton was co-founder and CEO of Asseta, a B2B marketplace for manufacturing equipment and spare parts. Asseta provides leading semiconductor manufacturers a safe and efficient platform to sell their excess assets. At Asseta, Anton raised money from premier investors such as Y Combinator, Gil Penchina, Jeff Epstein (ex-COO of Oracle), and Red Swan Ventures. Anton’s day-to-day focus was on business development. He led a global effort targeting Fortune 1000 companies across the US, Europe, China, Taiwan, Korea, Singapore, and Japan.

Anton is based in Austin and is passionate about being active and outdoors. He enjoys backpacking, beach volleyball, rock climbing, and swimming.
